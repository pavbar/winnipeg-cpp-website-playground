## Winnipeg C++ Developers - November 2025

![November 2025 Meetup](/images/event.jpeg)

Mark your calendars! Our November meetup is scheduled and we'd love to see you there.

### Event Details

**Date:** Thursday, November 20, 2025  
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM CST  
**Location:** Bill and Helen Norrie Library  
15 Poseidon Bay, Winnipeg, MB R3M 3E4

### What to Expect

Join us for an evening of C++ discussions, knowledge sharing, and networking with fellow developers from 
the Winnipeg area. This is a great opportunity to meet other C++ enthusiasts, learn something new, and 
contribute to our growing community.

Whether you're working on embedded systems, game development, high-performance computing, or enterprise 
applications, you'll find kindred spirits who understand the challenges and joys of working with C++.

### Registration

This is a free event, but please register on Eventbrite to help us plan for attendance:  
[Register on Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/winnipeg-c-developers-november-2025-tickets-1970002456378)

### Getting There

The Bill and Helen Norrie Library is located in the Whyte Ridge area of Winnipeg. Parking is available 
on-site. The library offers a comfortable meeting space perfect for our technical discussions.

### Questions?

Have questions about the meetup or the user group? Feel free to reach out through our Eventbrite page or 
connect with us at the event. We're here to build a welcoming community for all C++ developers in Winnipeg.

Looking forward to seeing you there!
